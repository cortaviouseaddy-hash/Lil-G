Lil-G assistant

Talk to Lil-G and hear it respond.

Type a message, tap the mic, or start wake listening and say "Hey Lil-G" if your browser supports speech recognition. With the desktop companion connected, Lil-G can also look at your screen and follow voice commands to click, tap, and type where you tell it.

Install it on your phone, tablet, laptop, or desktop

Chrome, Edge, and Android can show an install button. On iPhone or iPad, open this site in Safari, tap Share, then choose Add to Home Screen.

Settings Avatar, voice, memory, launch, and device controls

Voice and talk-back
